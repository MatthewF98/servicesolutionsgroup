What We Heard

The priorities you raised — and how BigChange delivers

Straight from our conversation on the demo, with the direct BigChange answer for each.

🗣️ What you said

"Joblogic is very focused on pre-planned maintenance. We're a lot more reactive. It just doesn't really suit what I want out of a CRM system."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

BigChange is built for reactive-first operations. Quick Job, Schedule Assist AI, route optimisation, drag-and-drop scheduling, hardwired tracking integrated into every decision. Reactive is the default — planned maintenance is the bonus.

🗣️ What you said

"Cash flow, very much so. That was historically one of the biggest issues with our previous Surface Medic franchise — getting payment off somebody when the technician left site."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

On-site card payments via Stripe — scan a QR code, customer pays before the technician leaves. Accepts Visa, Mastercard, Amex. Payment links in invoice emails with your branding. Cash collected same-day, not 30 days later.

🗣️ What you said

"We'll have a pre-scripted template for leak detection — they go through it methodically, complete all the sections, add narrative and photos. Can that be built in?"

✅ How BigChange fixes it

Fully customisable worksheets with conditional branching — yes/no, photo, free text, dropdowns, mandatory fields, signature capture. Speech-to-text for engineers so they can talk into the device. All time, date and GPS-stamped for evidence.

🗣️ What you said

"We're looking to push landing pages and quite unique, dynamic SEO campaigns — so having the ability for customers to book straight in would be even better."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

White-labelled booking site, fully branded with your franchise logos. Embed the booking widget directly into your landing pages. Customers request work, you accept, job auto-creates in the system and drops into the unscheduled bucket.

🗣️ What you said

"We have every kind of health and safety accreditation in the world here. Anything I can use to evidence that we do is very advantageous."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

Start-of-day H&S checklists, forced risk assessments before any job starts, PDF toolbox talks with read confirmation on the engineer's device. Every action time-stamped and signed off — audit-ready evidence for BFA scrutiny and franchise compliance.

🗣️ What you said

"I need a user-friendly CRM for field staff — something the technicians will actually use day-to-day without it slowing them down."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

The JobWatch app is built for usability. Big buttons, simple tap-through workflows, speech-to-text, one-tap SatNav. Technicians follow a forced path — timesheet, risk assessment, worksheet, sign-off — so nothing gets missed, but nothing gets in their way either.

🗣️ What you said

"Joblogic emails come from a Joblogic address — which is a pain because it ends up in spam, and it makes configuration very difficult."

✅ How BigChange fixes it

Every email sent — quotes, invoices, confirmations, customer satisfaction surveys, ETA alerts — comes from your franchise email domain. Fully white-labelled branding. No no-reply addresses landing in junk folders. Customers receive them, customers trust them.
